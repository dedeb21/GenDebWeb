Patriot Scholars Core Values

Servant Leadership
It inspires individuals to lead with humility and a selfless mindset, focusing on the well-being of the community and society. By prioritizing the greater good, it encourages the use of one’s abilities to serve others.
Professional Excellence
It fosters a commitment to achieving the highest standards of performance in one’s field. Beyond personal success, it emphasizes integrity, honor, and a sense of responsibility, particularly for DOST scholars who are entrusted with these principles.
Social Responsibility
It emphasizes the duty to contribute positively to society, especially by aiding those in need. This value promotes compassion, encourages social change, and inspires active engagement in community development.
